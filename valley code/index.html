About VOW

The Valley OF Welfare (VOW) is Community Based Organization founded in 2016. Led by Youth who are very committed to a continual and highly sprited national eradication of illiteracy among the elderly as well as the young.
We empower the young and the elderly, both in and out of school with relative skills, insights and opportunities they need to get education and realize their dreams. ...

We do this through a series of programs and activities that ensure initiation and sustainability of literacy programs,advocacy program and community's transformational projects that aim to mitigate the impact of illiteracy in the such as poverty.
The mission and work of the Valley Of Welfare are guided by the purposes and principles contained in its founding Charter.

Leadership Team

CHAIRPERSON

The Secretary-General of the Valley Of Welfare is a symbol of the Organization's ideals and a spokesman for the interests of the Community, in particular the poor and vulnerable. The current Secretary-General of the VOW, and the ninth occupant of the post, is Mr. SHUIB ABDI, who took office on 1 st September 2019. The VOW Charter describes the Secretary-General as "chief administrative officer" of the Organization.

VICE-CHAIRPERSON

The Secretary-General of the Valley Of Welfare is a symbol of the Organization's ideals and a spokesman for the interests of the Community, in particular the poor and vulnerable. The current Secretary-General of the VOW, and the ninth occupant of the post, is Mrs. KHADIJA AHMED who took office on 17th November 2019. The VOW Charter describes the Vice-Secretary-General as "chief administrative officer" of the Organization.

SECRETARY
